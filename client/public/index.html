A carbon footprint is defined as the total amount of greenhouse gases produced to directly and indirectly support a person's activities. It is important to remember that any estimation of a carbon footprint will have some level of inprecision- it is impossible to completely accurately estimate any one person's impact on the environment.

This application quantifies your carbon footprint using values that you submit for different aspects of your lifestyle. It also compares your carbon footprint with that of average citizens from different countries- these estimations are made based on the most recent (2014) figures for country per capita footprints, published by the World Bank.
